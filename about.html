About

My research asks how different forms of political organization have mediated the relationship between capitalism and the environment. Beyond this primary emphasis on environmental history, histories of capitalism, and comparative political economy, I am interested in architecture, empires, and how power flows through nature. I’m currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Weatherhead Scholars Program at Harvard University.

In my free time, I enjoy walking (definitely not running), traveling, and listening to all kinds of music.
